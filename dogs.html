Advantages of dogs

You can spend time/play with a dog. They make you more social. They can protect you from harm. They encourage you to move on. So dogs are like best friends.

Disadvantages of dogs

The biggest problem of dogs is that they poop everywhere -- From sofas to beds to public places to when you are walking with one. They chew stuff and accidently knock things over. They are very unhygenic. Keeping a dog outdoors would be better than indoors.
