Advantages of Cats

Cats are obedient pets. They are good to keep because, unlike dogs, they are clean. They stay quiet and can prevent loneliness. Therefore, they are good and cute companions.

Disadvantages of Cats

Cats shed alot. Their litter boxes require cleaning. They may claw your furniture to shreds. So keeping a cats' litter box in a place far away from the room you sleep, and keeping them indoors in a place away from furniture would be a good idea.
